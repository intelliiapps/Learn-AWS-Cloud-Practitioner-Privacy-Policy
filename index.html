1. Introduction

Welcome to the AWS Cloud Practitioner Learning App ("we," "our," or "the App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

By using the AWS Cloud Practitioner Learning App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use the App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

When you use our App, we may collect the following personal information:

  • Account Information: Email address, name, and profile picture (via Google Sign-In)
  • Authentication Data: Firebase Authentication user ID and authentication tokens
  • User Preferences: App settings and customization preferences

2.2 Learning Progress Data

To provide you with a personalized learning experience, we collect and store:

  • Quiz Scores: Your performance on quizzes and assessments
  • Topic Progress: Topics you have viewed and completed
  • Study Activity: Your learning patterns and content interaction
  • Achievements: Badges and milestones earned during your learning journey
  • Timestamps: When you completed activities and accessed content

2.3 Technical Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version
  • App Usage Data: Features used, time spent in the app
  • Crash Reports: Error logs and crash data (via Firebase Crashlytics)
  • Analytics Data: Anonymous usage statistics (via Firebase Analytics)
Note: We do NOT collect your exact location, contact list, camera/photo access, or any sensitive device information beyond what's necessary for app functionality.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Provide Services: Enable authentication, sync your progress across devices, and provide personalized learning experiences
  • Improve the App: Analyze usage patterns to enhance features and fix bugs
  • Progress Tracking: Store and display your quiz scores, completed topics, and achievements
  • Communication: Send important updates about the app (if you opt-in)
  • Security: Detect and prevent fraudulent activity and unauthorized access
  • Analytics: Understand how users interact with the app to improve user experience

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Cloud Storage

Your data is securely stored using Firebase Cloud Firestore and Firebase Authentication, which are industry-leading cloud platforms provided by Google. These services implement robust security measures including:

  • Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Encryption at rest
  • Regular security audits and compliance certifications
  • Secure data centers with 24/7 monitoring

4.2 Data Access Control

We implement strict access controls to ensure that only you can access your personal learning data. Your progress and quiz scores are isolated per user account and protected by Firebase Security Rules.

Security Commitment: While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We continuously work to improve our security measures.
